Who doesn't love a blend of alcohol and ice cream? And fresh from scratch soups, salads, sandwiches, and desserts? And sodas? Well, welcome to the Fountain on Locust where deliciousness meets fun. Voted Best Restaurant to Impress Out-of-Towners in the St. Louis Post Dispatch, the Fountain on Locust offers a plethora of great eats and bites. We are proud of our beautiful retro restaurant and our exceptional dishes, not to mention our friendly staff.

We are only a block away from Pappy's Smokehouse and five blocks from the fabulous Fox Theater and Powell Symphony Hall.
