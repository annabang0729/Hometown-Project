WHERE IS HOME FOR YOU?

When asked where your hometown is, can you immediately isolate one city or town? Or do you have several places that you may call home? What qualifies as "home" anyway? This project is meant to discover the different ways people respond to this seemingly simple question, "Where is home for you?" by describing their personal relationships with their hometown.
