PREFRONTAL CIRCUIT
NEXT>
The Prefrontal Circuit is
mainly responsible for daily cognitive tasks.
NEXT>
The DLPFC (dorsolateral prefrontal cortex) is
the main brain region of this circuit.
NEXT>
The DLPFC is activated across many tasks that
require attention, working memory, and cognitive control.
NEXT>
Solving a set of math problems, writing a brief
for a project, participating in discussion, or even playing a game
will be hindered if the DLPFC is not actviated properly.
NEXT>
Those suffering from depression consistently
show a hypoactive DLPFC when engaging in cognitive tasks.
< BACK TO FIRST PAGE
This pattern makes it very difficult for those with
depression to concentrate in seemingly simple tasks and causes them
to frequently make errors throughout the day.
