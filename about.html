Looking for the Best Ice Cream Bar and Restaurant in St. Louis?
Fountain On Locust, a one-of-a-kind cafe style restaurant and ice cream bar

Come visit The Fountain on Locust - the most photographed restaurant in St Louis, with a one-of-a-kind hand painted art deco interior surrounding the entire dining room! What makes this unique cafe restaurant and ice cream bar a must visit in St. Louis? Besides the beautiful retro restaurant interior, The Fountain on Locust is the Home of The Ice Cream Martini, exceptional soups, cafe style food, hand crafted cocktails and amazing ice cream desserts.
The Fountain's award winning desserts and drinks are a class above the usual ice cream parlors. Enjoy Signature Ice Cream Martinis, Old Fashioned Ice Cream Specialties, amazing adult desserts, Retro Cocktails, Champagne Floats, Classic Malts & Milkshakes and the best House-Made, Old-Fashioned Phosphate Sodas. Try some of the freshest bar drinks in The Fountains' mixologist style full bar, serving hand crafted Seasonal Cocktails, wine, beer, and 100% Lemon Lemonade.
