ABOUT
Depression is often mistaken for a personality defect or a prolonged mood state rather than an actual illness. While many understand depression in rather abstract terms, there is a strong biological and chemical basis that underlies the disorder. By highlighting the three major brain circuits that are dysfunctional among depressed individuals, THE DEPRESSED BRAIN attempts to eliminate social stigma surrounding depression and encourages people to treat it the same as any other disease.
